# What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of Computer Science that enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

Examples:

- Decision Making
- Pattern Recognition
- Learning from Data
- Problem Solving
- Language Understanding

---

# AI vs Traditional Programming

Traditional Programming

```text
Rules + Data
        │
        ▼
     Output
```

Artificial Intelligence

```text
Data + Correct Answers
           │
           ▼
      Machine Learning
           │
           ▼
      Learned Rules
           │
           ▼
      Predictions
```

# Automation vs AI

Automation

- Fixed Steps
- Never Learns
- Same Output
- Rule-Based

Examples

- ATM
- Calculator
- Washing Machine

Artificial Intelligence

- Learns
- Predicts
- Improves
- Handles New Situations

Examples

- ChatGPT
- Tesla Autopilot
- Netflix Recommendations

# Explainable AI (XAI)

Instead of saying:

```text
Loan Rejected
```

Explain:

```text
Loan Rejected

Reason:

Credit Score below required limit.
```

Good AI explains important decisions.

---

# Rule-Based AI

```text
IF

Salary > 30000

AND

Credit Score > 700

THEN

Approve Loan
```

Advantages

- Easy to build
- Easy to understand

Limitations

- Cannot learn
- Difficult to maintain
- Doesn't improve automatically

# Interview Questions

**Q1. What is AI?**

Building intelligent systems that can perform tasks requiring human intelligence.

---

**Q2. Difference between AI and Automation?**

Automation follows predefined rules.

AI learns and adapts.

---

**Q3. What is Explainable AI?**

AI that explains why a decision was made.

---

**Q4. What is Rule-Based AI?**

A system that uses manually written business rules.

---

**Q5. Why was Machine Learning introduced?**

To overcome the limitations of Rule-Based AI.
