# Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science and engineering of building
systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

This module lays the foundation for the entire learning journey. Before
learning Machine Learning, Deep Learning, LLMs, RAG, AI Agents and
Agentic AI, it is important to understand what AI is, why it exists, and
how it has evolved.

The goal of this module is to build intuition using simple Python
programs and one evolving project.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Learning Objectives

-   Explain AI in simple language.
-   Differentiate AI from traditional programming.
-   Understand Automation vs AI.
-   Explain the three types of AI.
-   Identify real-world AI applications.
-   Understand AI limitations.
-   Explain AI ethics and explainability.
-   Build a Rule-Based AI application.
-   Understand why Machine Learning became necessary.
