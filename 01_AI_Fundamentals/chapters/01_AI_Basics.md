# 🧠 What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of Computer Science that enables machines to perform tasks that normally require **human intelligence**.

Simply put,

> **AI tries to make computers think, learn and make decisions like humans.**

# 💡 Simple Example

Imagine a child.

You show the child 100 cats.

Later...

The child sees a new cat.

↓

The child immediately says

> "This is a cat."

How?

Because the child **learned from experience**.

AI works in a similar way.

Instead of experience,

AI learns from **data**.

# 🤔 Why was AI Created?

Imagine writing software for

Spam Detection.

Would you write rules like

```text
Contains "Free"

↓

Spam
```

What about

> Feel Free to Contact Me

Spam?

No.

Writing thousands of rules becomes impossible.

Instead,

AI studies millions of emails and learns the patterns automatically.

# 🎤 Interview Questions

## Q1. What is Artificial Intelligence?

**Answer**

Artificial Intelligence is the field of Computer Science that enables machines to perform tasks requiring human intelligence such as learning, reasoning and decision making.

## Q2. Is AI the same as Machine Learning?

No.

Machine Learning is one subset of Artificial Intelligence.
