# 📚 Types of AI

Artificial Intelligence is commonly divided into three categories.

```text
Artificial Intelligence
│
├── Narrow AI
├── General AI (AGI)
└── Super AI
```

---

# 1️⃣ Narrow AI (Weak AI)

Narrow AI is designed to perform **one specific task**.

It cannot perform tasks outside its area of expertise.

---

## Examples

- ChatGPT
- Siri
- Alexa
- Google Translate
- Netflix Recommendation
- Spam Detection
- Face Unlock
- Chess AI

---

### Example

```text
ChatGPT

↓

Answers Questions

↓

Cannot Drive a Car
```

ChatGPT is excellent at language tasks,

but it cannot perform every human activity.

---

# Characteristics

✅ Performs one task well.

✅ Trained for a specific purpose.

✅ Most AI today belongs here.

---

# 2️⃣ General AI (AGI)

AGI stands for **Artificial General Intelligence**.

The goal of AGI is to build AI that can perform **any intellectual task** that a human can.

---

### Example

A human can:

- Write code
- Cook
- Drive
- Learn music
- Solve maths
- Paint
- Play cricket

An AGI system should be able to perform all these tasks without being specially programmed for each one.

---

### Current Status

```text
Research

↓

Not Yet Achieved
```

No true AGI exists today.

---

# Characteristics

✅ Learns any task.

✅ Human-level intelligence.

✅ Can transfer knowledge between domains.

---

# 3️⃣ Super AI

Super AI is a hypothetical AI that would be **more intelligent than humans** in every field.

It would outperform humans in:

- Science
- Medicine
- Programming
- Creativity
- Research
- Decision Making

---

### Current Status

```text
Theoretical

↓

Does Not Exist
```

Super AI is still discussed mainly in research and science fiction.

---

# 📊 Comparison

| Feature | Narrow AI | AGI | Super AI |
|----------|------------|-----|-----------|
| Exists Today | ✅ Yes | ❌ No | ❌ No |
| Performs One Task | ✅ Yes | ❌ No | ❌ No |
| Human-Level Intelligence | ❌ No | ✅ Yes | Beyond Humans |
| Learns Multiple Domains | Limited | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |

---

# 🌍 Real-World Examples

|-------------|----------|
