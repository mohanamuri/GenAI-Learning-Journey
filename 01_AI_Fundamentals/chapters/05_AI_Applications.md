# 💼 AI Engineer Note

Every AI project begins with a business problem.

Not with an algorithm.

The process is usually:

```text
Business Problem

↓

Collect Data

↓

Choose AI Technique

↓

Train Model

↓

Deploy

↓

Monitor

↓

Improve
```

Always understand the problem first.
