# 🤔 Why Should You Learn This?

Many beginners think:

> AI can solve every problem.

This is not true.

Even the most advanced AI systems have limitations.

As an AI Engineer, you should know **when AI is suitable and when it is not.**

---

# 🧠 AI Depends on Data

AI learns from historical data.

If the data is poor,

the predictions will also be poor.

This is often summarized as:

```text
Garbage In

↓

Garbage Out
```

Good Data → Good AI

Bad Data → Bad AI

---

# 🌍 Example

Suppose a loan approval system was trained using incorrect customer records.

```text
Wrong Data

↓

Wrong Learning

↓

Wrong Prediction
```

The AI is not at fault.

The data is.

---

# 📊 Limitation 1 - Data Dependency

AI cannot learn without data.

```text
No Data

↓

No Learning

↓

No Prediction
```

---

# 📊 Limitation 2 - Bias

AI can learn human bias.

Example:

If historical hiring data contains bias,

AI may continue making biased hiring decisions.

```text
Biased Data

↓

Biased Model

↓

Biased Prediction
```

---

# 📊 Limitation 3 - No Common Sense

Humans understand context naturally.

AI may not.

Example:

```text
Can an elephant fit inside a refrigerator?
```

Humans know the answer instantly.

AI depends on training and reasoning.

---

# 📊 Limitation 4 - No Emotions

Humans understand:

- Happiness
- Sadness
- Anger
- Empathy

AI does not truly experience emotions.

It only predicts suitable responses.

---

# 📊 Limitation 5 - AI Can Be Wrong

AI makes predictions.

Predictions are not guarantees.

Example

```text
Medical Diagnosis

↓

AI says

95% Probability
```

It does not mean

100% Correct.

Doctors still make the final decision.

---

# 📊 Limitation 6 - Expensive

Building AI systems requires:

- Data
- GPUs
- Cloud Infrastructure
- Storage
- Engineers
- Continuous Monitoring

Large AI systems are expensive to build and maintain.

---

# 📊 Limitation 7 - Security Risks

AI systems can be attacked.

Examples

- Fake Images
- Deepfakes
- Prompt Injection
- Data Poisoning

Security is an important part of AI engineering.

---

# 📊 AI Limitations Overview

```text
AI

│

├── Needs Good Data

├── Can Be Biased

├── No Emotions

├── Limited Common Sense

├── Can Be Wrong

├── Expensive

└── Security Risks
```

---

# 🏦 Our Project

Imagine our Loan Eligibility System.

If we train the model using incorrect customer records,

the model may approve risky customers

or reject good customers.

```text
Poor Dataset

↓

Poor Model

↓

Poor Loan Decisions
```

This is why data quality is critical.

---

# 💼 AI Engineer Note

An AI Engineer spends more time on:

- Data Cleaning
- Data Validation
- Feature Engineering
- Model Evaluation

than on training the model itself.

A good model cannot fix bad data.

---

# ⚠ Common Mistakes

❌ AI is always correct.

✔ AI predicts based on available data.

---

❌ More AI means better results.

✔ Better data leads to better results.

---

❌ AI replaces humans completely.

✔ Human review is still essential in many critical applications.

---

# 🎤 Interview Questions

## Q1. What is the biggest limitation of AI?

AI depends heavily on the quality of data.

---

## Q2. What is Bias in AI?

Bias occurs when AI learns unfair or unbalanced patterns from historical data.

---

## Q3. Does AI have emotions?

No.

AI predicts responses but does not truly experience emotions.

---

## Q4. Why is human supervision important?

Because AI predictions can be incorrect, especially in critical areas such as healthcare, banking and law.

# ⭐ Must Remember

✅ AI is only as good as its data.

✅ AI can be biased.

✅ AI has no emotions.

✅ AI predicts, not guarantees.

✅ Human supervision is important.
