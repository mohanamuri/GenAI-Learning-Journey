# Question 1

## What is Artificial Intelligence?

### Answer

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of Computer Science that enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, decision-making and problem-solving.

Example:

- ChatGPT
- Google Maps
- Netflix Recommendations

---

# Question 2

## What is the difference between AI and Traditional Programming?

### Answer

Traditional Programming follows manually written rules.

Artificial Intelligence learns patterns from historical data and makes predictions.

---

# Question 3

## Difference between Automation and AI?

| Automation | AI |
|------------|----|
| Fixed Rules | Learns from Data |
| Same Output | Predictions |
| No Learning | Continuous Learning |

---

# Question 4

## What are the three types of AI?

Answer

1. Narrow AI
2. General AI (AGI)
3. Super AI

Only Narrow AI exists today.

---

# Question 5

## Is ChatGPT AGI?

No.

ChatGPT is an example of Narrow AI.

It is highly capable but still designed for specific classes of tasks.

# Question 9

## What is Explainable AI (XAI)?

Explainable AI helps users understand why an AI system made a particular decision.

Example

```text
Loan Rejected

Reason

↓

Credit Score below required limit
```

---

# Question 10

## What is Rule-Based AI?

Rule-Based AI is an AI system that makes decisions using manually written IF–ELSE rules.

Example

```python
if salary >= 30000:
    print("Approved")
```

---

# Question 11

## Why was Machine Learning introduced?

Writing and maintaining thousands of business rules became difficult.

Machine Learning automatically learns patterns from historical data.

# Question 14

## Why is modular architecture important?

Because individual components can evolve independently.

Example

```text
Validation

↓

No Change

Decision Engine

↓

Rule-Based AI

↓

Machine Learning

↓

Deep Learning
```

Only one component changes.
