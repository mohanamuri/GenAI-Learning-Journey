# Question 1

## What is Artificial Intelligence?

### Answer

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of Computer Science that enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, decision-making and problem-solving.

Example:

- ChatGPT
- Google Maps
- Netflix Recommendations

---

# Question 2

## What is the difference between AI and Traditional Programming?

### Answer

Traditional Programming follows manually written rules.

Artificial Intelligence learns patterns from historical data and makes predictions.

---

# Question 3

## Difference between Automation and AI?

| Automation | AI |
|------------|----|
| Fixed Rules | Learns from Data |
| Same Output | Predictions |
| No Learning | Continuous Learning |

---

# Question 4

## What are the three types of AI?

Answer

1. Narrow AI
2. General AI (AGI)
3. Super AI

Only Narrow AI exists today.

---

# Question 5

## Is ChatGPT AGI?

No.

ChatGPT is an example of Narrow AI.

It is highly capable but still designed for specific classes of tasks.
