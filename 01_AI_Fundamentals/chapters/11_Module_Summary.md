# ⭐ Top 10 Things to Remember

### 1.

AI is a broad field of Computer Science.

---

### 2.

Machine Learning is a subset of AI.

---

### 3.

Automation follows predefined rules.

AI learns from data.

---

### 4.

Most AI systems today are Narrow AI.

---

### 5.

ChatGPT is Narrow AI.

Not AGI.

---

### 6.

AI predictions are not guarantees.

---

### 7.

AI depends on good quality data.

---

### 8.

AI should be Fair, Transparent and Explainable.

---

### 9.

Rule-Based AI is still useful.

Not every problem needs Machine Learning.

---

### 10.

Always understand the business problem before choosing an AI solution.
