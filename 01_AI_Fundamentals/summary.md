# AI Fundamentals - Quick Revision

## Intelligence

Learning from experience and using learned patterns to make better decisions.

---

## Artificial Intelligence

Computers learning patterns from data instead of following only predefined rules.

---

## Traditional Programming

```
Human

↓

↓

```

---

## Artificial Intelligence

```
Human
```

## Rule-Based Programming

Developers define every business rule.

Example

```
Age >= 18

↓

Adult
```

---

## Pattern Matching

Uses multiple conditions to make decisions.

Example

Country + Age

↓

Adult / Minor

---

## Decision Engine

Uses multiple business rules.

Example

Country

Age

↓

Example
