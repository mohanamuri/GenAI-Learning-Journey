# What is Intelligence?
Intelligence is the ability to learn patterns from experience and use those patterns to make decisions in new situations.

From Experience --> Learn Pattern --> Apply Pattern
## Learning Objectives
After completing this lesson, we will be able to:

- Understand what intelligence means.
- Explain why learning is important.
- Differentiate intelligence from automation.
- Explain why a calculator is not AI.

## Why is Intelligence Important?
Intelligence is the foundation for AI.

## Calculator vs Human
Calculator solves the problems and cannot learn patterns or any thing new.
Humans can learn new things.
A calculator is fast and accurate, but it cannot learn or improve from experience.

Calculator is NOT EQUAL Intelligence.
Calculator is smart.
But Remember, BEING SMART is != Being Intelligence

## Key Characteristics of Intelligence

Characteristics of Intelligence

• Learns from experience

• Improves over time
